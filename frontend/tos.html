Terms of Service

Section 1: Hardware Integrity Policy

InkChat is designed and optimized exclusively for native e-ink hardware terminals. Attempting to bypass, spoof, or modify device identifiers, canvas rendering profiles, or client-side telemetry attributes violates our platform integrity guidelines. Any session found in violation of native hardware validations will be denied immediate access.

Section 2: Anti-Spam & Conduct Rules

To preserve robust philosophical debate, human interaction is required. Automated script transmissions, duplicate flooding, and abusive profile names are prohibited. Violations of system guidelines will result in the immediate revocation of workspace access privileges.

Section 3: Access Control and Audits

The platform monitors validation logs silently to protect system health. All access lockouts are enforced dynamically at the session level without network level disruptions for compliant nodes.
